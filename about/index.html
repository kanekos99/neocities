Hello and welcome to my page! I go by Kanekos or Kaneko online. I am really bad at writing self introductions because I never know what to put in these so I’m sorry if this sounds like some kind of cover letter for a job application.

My main hobby is drawing and I mostly draw my own original characters. To view my art, you can check out my gallery page! I’m currently in the process of trying to make a dedicated OC page for my characters, but it is a complicated project due to the complexity of organising them, and Toyhou.se already offers all the features I could every want as an archive for my OCs.

I'm really interested in game dev and my goal is to make a completed indie game. I have several wip RPGMaker games and I’m trying to find some time to learn Godot. However, I also have too many projects and a very bad track record of completing them.

When not drawing or working on my personal projects, I spend the majority of my time playing Gacha games. I’ve played quite a number of Gacha games but currently Honkai Star Rail is at the forefront of my interests. I tend to hyper-fixate on certain fictional characters for a period of time and that basically becomes my whole personality until I get over them (or find someone else to hyper fixate on).

I also love cosy games that involve decorationg/ building/ dress-up. I have an unhealthy habit of playing Animal Crossing nonstop for several days before dropping it altogether and then picking it back up again months later for the cycle to start all over again.
